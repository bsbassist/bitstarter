## Development Process

For nontrivial changes (new features, behavior changes, anything touching
production config, credentials, or deployment), open a PR and let Brent
review before merging — don't push directly to `master`. Small fixes
(typos, obvious bugs, doc tweaks) can go straight to `master`.

If you open a branch and PR, follow through: don't leave it hanging
unmerged and unmentioned. Either get it merged or explicitly flag it to
Brent as abandoned, and why.
